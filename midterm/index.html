In My Element

Literature has been a consistent source of comfort and refuge for me as I have grown and navigated and continue to grow and navigate

an everchanging world. Back to even my earliest memories I have used words as shelters, anchors, and means of expression. While I never

stuck to writing, some of the hardest times of my life have been compartmentalized in line breaks and tinted paper. Stories, poems, books,

and lyrics are the lens through which I see the world. These words transformed from thoughts into tangible, visible books and letters

saturate my heart and mind with so much more than I could ever give back to these art forms and the authors that create within them.
