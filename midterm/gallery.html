Here are some book I have read recently. These books are all over the place from an autobiography (Educated) to a book about mental health that covers difficult topics (Girl in pieces) to a murder mystery series (A Good Girl's Guide to Murder). One book in particular I recomend is The Girl who Circumnavigated Fairyland in a Ship of her own Making. This book follows the protagonist, September, who finds herself transported to a quirky, unconventional fairyland on the back of the Green Wind. The 12-year-old collects eccentric friends on a journey that left me in wonder when I read the last few chapters of this endearing novel.
