Welcome to Madison Books, the neighborhood bookstore for Madison Park and its environs in Seattle.

As of March 12, 2022, state mandates no longer require that customers be masked, though we continue to recommend that masks be worn, regardless of your vaccination status. We continue to offer front-door pickup during all of our hours of operation, and can arrange shipping or contactless delivery as well. Stop by, email us at info@madisonbks.com, or order for mailing online. Thanks for continuing to support us!

Events

We’re pleased to bring to our readers a series of literary events featuring some of the most interesting authors from the Northwest and beyond. Join us for stimulating discussion about books and much more. The full schedule and details about every event are on our calendar. We also co-host a regional series of online author events called Books in Common NW; the full schedule is available on the BICNW calendar.

Our Newsletter

Periodically, we send out an email newsletter containing book recommendations, store news, and any other book-related thing we think you might want to know about. If you’d like to join our mailing list (we won’t spam you otherwise), you can do so here.

Phinney Books

Don’t forget to visit our sibling store in Seattle’s Greenwood/Phinney Ridge neighborhood, Phinney Books. Pay a call in person at 7405 Greenwood Ave. N or find their presence online at phinneybooks.com.
