Why This Website?

An integral part of Washington and the Greater Seattle Area's culture is its nature and specifically it's lakes. In this web design project, we hoped to focus on some of the natural history/heritage of our local lakes. Though such a prominent part of west-Washington's geography, the history of the lakes is often less focused on. Because of the rapid urbanization of the Great Seattle Area, the output and result of that has been an absurd increase in the pollution of our lakes. Our intent of this website was to spread awareness and to expose people to the history, current state, and volunteer events of our lakes.
