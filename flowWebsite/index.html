What is my flow?

It is hard to ask myself what my flow is.

I have never been able to maintain a interest in any specific sports, arts, music, or media for long.
Maybe that isn't a unique experience but still, it resonates with me when prompted with this question.
Having said that, there is something fascinating with the manipulation of self. With makeup, clothing,
piercings, and changing of my normal state of being, I am enraptured. Maybe it is narcissistic to stare,
fully engaged, at myself in a mirror of hours, I am truley in my flow practicing makeup, and using myself
as a large dress up doll.
